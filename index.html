Mahalo Privacy Policy

Summary

The Mahalo app transfers data to and from the Twitch video platform. It may transfer technical information about a user's system to Hockeyapp and Microsoft for the purposes of error reporting and app analytics.

Third-party services

  • Mahalo is a client for the Twitch video platform, and so may transfer information to and from the Twitch service. The Twitch privacy policy is available here: https://www.twitch.tv/p/privacy-policy
  • Mahalo uses Hockeyapp to collect crash logs. Crash logs are automatically uploaded if the app crashes, and include the model of your device and your OS version. The Hockeyapp privacy policy is available here: https://privacy.microsoft.com/en-us/privacystatement
  • Microsoft may provide Mahalo with Error Reporting and App Analytics data. This data is subject to the App Developer Agreement, Section 9. https://msdn.microsoft.com/en-us/windows/apps/hh694058.aspx
